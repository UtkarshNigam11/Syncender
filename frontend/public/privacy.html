Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Syncender ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our sports calendar integration service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Syncender, we collect:

  • Google Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture
  • Google Calendar Data: Access to create, read, and modify calendar events
  • User Preferences: Favorite teams, sports, and notification settings
  • Usage Data: App interactions and feature usage

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data ONLY to:

  • Sync sports match schedules to your Google Calendar
  • Send you match notifications and reminders
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your calendar events with anyone
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising purposes
  • ❌ Access your calendar events not created by Syncender

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure token storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data retention

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Revoke calendar access permissions
  • Export your data

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke Syncender's access to your Google Calendar at any time by visiting:
https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Syncender is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:
Email: privacy@syncender.com
Support: support@syncender.com
